MICHELLE AIKO HIGA
My Work About Me

Michelle Aiko Higa is a graphic designer, painter and student of medicine. In 2021, she graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a two degree: Integrative Human Biology and Global Poverty and Practice.

With a vast set of skills ranging from digital, sketch, watercolor, acrylic, Michelle draws inspiration from her Japanese heritage to create pieces of both personal and societal value. She is continuously looking for ways to bridge healthcare and education with her design skills to impact indigenous and marginalized communities.
